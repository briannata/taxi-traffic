Taxi Traffic

An algorithm to recognize, display, and predict highest areas of demand for taxis.

Developed by Brianna Ta, Riya Boyapati, and Sreenidhi Sankararaman.

DESCRIPTION

What does it do?

Our Taxi Traffic algorithm recognizes, displays, and predicts the areas of high taxi demand in DC during various time intervals for a specific day.

How was it made?

Using Jupyter Notebooks, we analyzed taxi data from each month of 2019 and outputted our analysis and predictions onto a map of DC.

How does it work?

Our script performs the k-means clustering algorithm on the data to find the hotspots of taxi activity. We then analyze how many taxis were in each cluster during a specific time interval. Lastly, using this data, we predict taxi demand for future months using a robust nonlinear least squares regression algorithm.

Algorithm Display

Select the year, month, day, and time, and the Taxi Traffic algorithm will generate a map of the taxi data analysis and predictions at that specific time.
